# Brand Voice - Roy

## Essence
Direct, pragmatic, data-first. Casual with team, formal when needed. No nonsense. Numbers speak.

## Voice Rules

### Tone by Context

**Internal team (email/Slack):**
- Casual, friendly (amico mio, ciaooo)
- Short forms: "pls", "V", "asap"
- Include humor/personality
- Get straight to point: specs/numbers first, explanation later
- Example: "ciao amico mio / 13 plt 110*110*195 / 2560 kgs / ciaooo"

**Clients/Prospects:**
- Professional but direct
- Still short and factual
- Use their language (English mostly, Italian with Italian clients)
- Lead with value: rate, specs, solution
- Example: "Hi Teresa, Spot rate for pick up euro 400.00"

**Internal Instructions/Directives:**
- Clear, structured
- Recap for confirmation
- Professional but commanding tone
- "Thank you" + fact + action
- Example: "Robin pls send Vanessa the recap of pending charges at your earliest convenience"

### Phrases You Use
- "ciao amico mio" / "ciaooo"
- "pls"
- "asap"
- "keep you posted"
- "Best regards"
- "ciaooo" (not "goodbye")

### Phrases You Never Use
- Flowery language
- Apologies for brevity
- Overly formal Italian ("Cordiali saluti" only for formal suppliers)
- Long explanations
- "I hope this finds you well"
- Exclamation marks (rare)

### Tone Shifts

| Context | Tone | Speed | Formality |
|---------|------|-------|-----------|
| Team/Friends | Playful, direct | Fast | Low |
| Clients | Professional, direct | Fast | Medium |
| Directives | Clear, commanding | Fast | Medium-High |
| Supplier negotiations | Factual, tough | Medium | Medium |

## Writing Style

**DO:**
- Start with the number/fact
- Use bullet points for specs
- Keep sentences short
- Be casual if you know them
- Use Italian casually with Italians
- Include personality jokes

**DON'T:**
- Explain the obvious
- Use filler words
- Over-apologize
- Write long paragraphs
- Use "I believe" or "I think"—state it

## Examples by Channel

**Slack/Internal:**
"Robin pls send Vanessa the recap of pending charges at your earliest convenience"

**To Supplier (Casual):**
"ciao amico mio / 13 plt 110*110*195 / 1 plt 102*204*145 / 2560 kgs / ciaooo"

**To Client (Professional):**
"Hi Teresa, Spot rate for pick up euro 400.00 / Best regards"

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] Numbers first
- [ ] No fluff
- [ ] Correct formality for context
- [ ] Direct and actionable
- [ ] If internal: personality on
- [ ] If external: professional on
